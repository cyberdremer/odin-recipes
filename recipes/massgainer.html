MassGainer Shake

Description

The MassGainer Shake is a protein shake that is designed to help you bulk without causing serious damage to your health. It contains many health ingredients that make it a no brainer to add to your lineup for things to eat on your bulking journey.
Be warned, this is not for the lactose intolerant.

Ingredients

  • Whole Milk
  • One Large Banana
  • Two Cups of Blueberries
  • One tbsp of peanut butter
  • One tbsp of greek yogurt
  • Two cups of rolled oats
  • One scoop of whey protein
  • One scoop of creatine

Directions

  1. Put all the aformentioned ingredients into a Ninja blender.
  2. Plug in the base of the blender
  3. Attach the blender container to the base of the blender
  4. Use the extract option
  5. Wait until it has blended
  6. Serve in a cup and enjoy the 1000+ calorie behemoth of a drink
